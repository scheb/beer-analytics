White Labs German Ale/Kölsch (WLP029) is a liquid ale yeast that has a medium attenuation of 75.0%, medium flocculation, and medium alcohol tolerance. The ideal fermentation temperature for this yeast is 18.0-21.0 °C, making it great for lagers and ales alike. White Labs German Ale/Kölsch is perfect for producing traditional German-style Kölsch, Düsseldorf Altbier, Cream Ale, Blonde Ale and other light ales. It offers a clean flavor profile with subtle fruitiness and low esters.
