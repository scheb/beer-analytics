White Labs German Ale II (WLP003) is a versatile liquid ale yeast, perfect for creating classic German ales. It has an attenuation of 76.5%, meaning it will ferment out the sugars in the wort quickly and completely, resulting in a beer with a dry finish and good clarity. The flocculation is medium, providing enough sedimentation to give your beer good body but not too much that it becomes cloudy.
