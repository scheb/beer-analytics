Wyeast's West Yorkshire (1469) is a liquid ale yeast strain that offers excellent attenuation at 69.0% and high flocculation. It has an alcohol tolerance up to 9.0%, making it ideal for stronger beer styles, with an ideal fermentation temperature of 18.0-22.0 °C. Common beer styles brewed with this strain include English bitters, milds, old ales, porters and stouts, where its flavor profile provides a balance of subtle fruit esters and malty sweetness. This strain can also be used to brew German altbiers and Kölsch beers, offering a crisp finish with mild hop bitterness.
