The Wild Thing of Escarpment Labs is a liquid ale yeast that has an attenuation of 78.5%, flocculation of medium, alcohol tolerance up to 9.0%, and is ideal for fermentation temperatures between 25.0-25.0 °C. This strain has been proven to provide the perfect balance of fruity and estery flavours, making it perfect for producing a variety of ales with complex characters. It’s also known for its reliable attenuation and ability to finish cleanly, providing brewers with consistent results each time they use this yeast strain.
