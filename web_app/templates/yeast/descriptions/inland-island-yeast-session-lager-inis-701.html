Session Lager (INIS 701) from Inland Island Yeast is a liquid lager yeast strain with a medium flocculation and alcohol tolerance. It has an attenuation of 77.5%, meaning that it converts sugars into alcohol and carbon dioxide efficiently. Session Lager is best used when fermented between 10-13°C, which allows for the production of delicious malty lagers with a smooth finish. This yeast can also be used to brew wheat beers and Kölsch styles as well.
