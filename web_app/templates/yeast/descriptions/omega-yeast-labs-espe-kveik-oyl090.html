The Espe Kveik (OYL090) from Omega Yeast Labs is a liquid ale yeast. It has a high attenuation of 77.0%, a medium flocculation, and can tolerate high levels of alcohol. It ferments best at temperatures between 20.0-37.0 degrees Celsius, making it an ideal choice for brewing ales in a wide range of temperatures. This yeast strain produces an array of fruity esters and is great for creating complex flavor profiles in your beer!
