Wyeast’s American Ale (1056) is a liquid ale yeast that has medium-low flocculation and high attenuation of 75.0%. This strain has an alcohol tolerance of 10.0% and an ideal fermentation temperature of 16.0-22.0°C, making it an excellent choice for high-alcohol beers. Popular beer styles that can be brewed with this yeast include American pale ales, American amber ales and IPAs. This yeast will impart a slightly fruity flavor profile with hints of apple and pear to the finished beer.
