Urkel (L28) is a liquid lager yeast from Imperial Yeast. It has an attenuation of 73.0%, flocculation of medium, and an ideal fermentation temperature range of 11.0-14.0 °C. This yeast produces beers with clean malt flavor and low levels of esters, giving it a crisp taste with a subtle hint of fruitiness. It is best used for light lagers such as pilsners, helles, kölschs, and märzens.
