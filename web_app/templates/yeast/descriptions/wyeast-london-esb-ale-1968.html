Wyeast's London ESB Ale (1968) is a liquid ale yeast that is perfect for producing classic English style ales. Featuring very high flocculation and an attenuation of 69.0%, this strain has an alcohol tolerance of 10% and ferments best at temperatures between 18-22 degrees Celsius. Common beer styles brewed with this yeast include Extra Special Bitters, English Pale Ales, Strong Ales, and Barleywines. The flavor profile imparted by this strain is highly complex and includes notes of fruitiness, nuttiness, earthiness, spice, and toffee-like sweetness.
