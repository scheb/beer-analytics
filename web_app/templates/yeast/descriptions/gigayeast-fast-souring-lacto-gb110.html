The GigaYeast Fast Souring Lacto (GB110) is a liquid strain of either brettanomyces or bacteria. It has an attenuation of 40%, meaning it can convert 40% of the available sugars into alcohol and CO2. The ideal fermentation temperature for this strain is between 20-37°C (68-98°F). This strain will produce a sour beer with a crisp, tart finish that's perfect for any summer day.
