Loki (A43) is a liquid ale yeast strain produced by Imperial Yeast. This strain has an attenuation of 80.0% and produces medium-high flocculation, which makes it ideal for producing beers with both clarity and body. It can be used between 16.0-38.0 Â°C, making it versatile enough to produce a wide variety of beer styles. Loki is perfect for creating American pale ales, IPAs, American stouts, American wheat beers and American lagers because of its neutral flavor profile that allows the hop character to come through without any other characteristics being added. The resulting beers have a crisp and clean finish that makes them easy to drink and highly enjoyable.
