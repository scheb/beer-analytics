The Brettanomyces Bruxellensis (OYL-202) from Omega Yeast Labs is a liquid pitch suitable for making sour beers. It is a type of brett or bacteria, with an attenuation rate of 48.0%, low flocculation and an alcohol tolerance of 10.0%. This yeast works best at temperatures between 20.0 and 29.0 degrees Celsius, and can be used to create complex and nuanced flavors in your beer.
