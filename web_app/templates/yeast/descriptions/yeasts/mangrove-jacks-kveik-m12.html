Mangrove Jack's Kveik (M12) is a dry ale yeast strain that is well-suited for brewing a wide variety of beer styles. It has high flocculation and a high alcohol tolerance, making it suitable for brewing high-alcohol beers. Ideal fermentation temperatures range from 20.0°C to 40.0°C, making it an ideal choice for brewers in hot climates. The unique flavor profile of this strain adds notes of tropical fruit, citrus, and spice to the finished beer. It can be used to brew a wide range of styles including IPAs, saisons, sours, Belgian ales, and many more.
