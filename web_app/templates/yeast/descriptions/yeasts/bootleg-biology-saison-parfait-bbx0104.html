The Saison Parfait (BBX0104) liquid yeast from Bootleg Biology is an ale yeast type with a medium flocculation. It has a high attenuation rate of 90.0%, and an alcohol tolerance of medium. The ideal fermentation temperature for this yeast strain is 18.0-29.0 °C, making it suitable for brewing saisons, farmhouse ales, bières de garde, and Belgian styles. This strain produces a clean profile with moderate fruity esters and spicy phenols, adding complexity to many beer styles.
