Omega Yeast Labs' West Coast Ale III (OYL-043) is a liquid ale yeast with a medium-high flocculation rate. It has an attenuation level of 80%, allowing for a highly fermentable beer, and an alcohol tolerance of 11%. The ideal fermentation temperature for this strain is between 18 to 20 degrees Celsius, making it suitable for use in a variety of brewing conditions. This strain is well suited for those looking to brew a classic West Coast style ale with balanced bitterness and a strong hop character.
