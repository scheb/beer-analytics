The Kolsch II (OYL-044) ale yeast from Omega Yeast Labs is a versatile liquid yeast strain with medium flocculation and high attenuation. It is suitable for creating a wide range of ales, including Kölsch, cream ale, and blonde ales. This strain has an alcohol tolerance of up to 10%, and it ferments best at 18°C - 21°C. Kolsch II produces a clean profile with low ester formation and is great for creating crisp, clean beers without overpowering flavors or aromas.
