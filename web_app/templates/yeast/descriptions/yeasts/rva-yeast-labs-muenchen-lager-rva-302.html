München Lager (RVA 302) from RVA Yeast Labs is a liquid lager yeast with high flocculation and medium alcohol tolerance. It has an attenuation of 77.5%, meaning it can convert a significant amount of sugar into alcohol and carbon dioxide, making it ideal for lagers. The optimal fermentation temperature range for this yeast strain is 14.0-18.0 °C, making it suitable to brew in cooler temperatures that are typical of lagers.
