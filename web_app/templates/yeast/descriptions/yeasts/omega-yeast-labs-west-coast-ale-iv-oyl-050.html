West Coast Ale IV (OYL-050) is a liquid ale yeast strain released by Omega Yeast Labs. This strain has a high attenuation of 77%, and a medium-high flocculation rate. It also has an impressive alcohol tolerance of 10%, allowing for easy production of high ABV beer styles. Additionally, this strain is best used at temperatures between 17 and 23 degrees Celsius, making it ideal for fermenting in warmer climates.
