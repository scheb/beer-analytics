The Kolsch I (OYL-017) liquid ale yeast from Omega Yeast Labs is a top-fermenting strain that produces crisp, clean beers. This yeast is known for its low flocculation and high attenuation, allowing it to achieve a final gravity as low as 1.008. It has an alcohol tolerance of up to 10% ABV, making it an excellent choice for strong ales and lagers. This yeast ferments best at temperatures between 13 and 21 degrees Celsius, resulting in smooth and complex flavor profiles.
