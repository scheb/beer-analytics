The Trapeze Trappist (CL009) by Crescat Labs is a liquid ale yeast for fermentation. It has a medium flocculation rate, with an average attenuation of 76.0%. Its alcohol tolerance is up to 12.0% and it ferments best at temperatures between 19.0-25.0 °C. This strain of yeast produces a light coloured beer with good body and mild esters – ideal for any Belgian style ale!
