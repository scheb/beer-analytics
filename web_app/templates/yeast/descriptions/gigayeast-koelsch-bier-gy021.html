GigaYeast's Kölsch Bier (GY021) is a liquid ale yeast strain that is perfect for brewing German-style Kölsch beers. It has an attenuation of 81.0% and a medium-high flocculation, making it easy to work with and achieve consistent results. This strain is ideal for fermentation temperatures between 16.0 and 22.0 °C, producing a clean and crisp flavor that accentuates the malt character of the beer. With its balanced characteristics, Kölsch Bier (GY021) makes for an excellent choice for any homebrewer looking to brew up classic German styles.
