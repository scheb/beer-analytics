The Saison : Baron (INIS-298) liquid ale yeast from Inland Island Yeast is a great option for creating complex, flavorful beers. This yeast strain has an attenuation of 75.0%, meaning that most of the sugars in the wort are converted into alcohol during fermentation and produces a dry beer with medium body. Flocculation is medium, which means the yeast will settle out of suspension quickly once fermentation is finished.
