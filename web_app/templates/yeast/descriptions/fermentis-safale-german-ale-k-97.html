Fermentis' Safale German Ale (K-97) is a dry ale yeast that is ideal for creating crisp and refreshing beer styles. Its high flocculation rate makes it easy to separate from the beverage, while its 81.0% attenuation ensures that the flavors of your brew will be fully realized. It has an impressive alcohol tolerance of 10.4%, and it ferments best at temperatures within the range of 12.0-25.0 °C. This ale yeast produces a clean flavor profile with slightly fruity undertones, making it an excellent choice for brewing traditional German styles such as Kölsch, Altbier, and Weizenbock. It can also be used to craft many other styles of beer including Pilsners, India Pale Ales, and Belgian ales.
