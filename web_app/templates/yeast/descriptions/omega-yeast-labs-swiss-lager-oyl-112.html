Omega Yeast Labs' Swiss Lager (OYL-112) is a liquid lager yeast that offers a high attenuation of 75.0%. It has medium flocculation, low alcohol tolerance and an ideal fermentation temperature between 10.0-13.0 °C. This lager yeast is great for homebrewers looking to create crisp, clean-tasting lagers with a balanced malt and hop profile. This versatile strain can also be used to make wheat beers, Belgian strong ales and Kölschs.
