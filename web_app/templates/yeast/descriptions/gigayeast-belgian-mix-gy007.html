GigaYeast's Belgian Mix (GY007) is a liquid ale yeast ideal for Belgian-style ales and beers. It has an attenuation of 80.0%, a low flocculation rate, and is best fermented at a temperature range of 19.0 to 25.0 °C (66-77 °F). This yeast strain contains two Belgian strains that provide distinct ester production characteristics, allowing brewers to express the unique character of their beers. The Belgian Mix also adds subtle spice nuances from clove and pepper to the beer's aroma and flavor profile.
