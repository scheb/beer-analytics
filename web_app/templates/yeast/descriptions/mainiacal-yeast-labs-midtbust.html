Midtbust from Mainiacal Yeast Labs is a liquid ale yeast that offers an impressive 78.0% attenuation rate and medium flocculation and alcohol tolerance. This strain of yeast is ideal for fermentation temperatures between 24.0°C - 41.0°C, making it an excellent choice for those looking to brew ales and lagers alike. Midtbust can help create beers with a balanced flavour profile, making it a great all-rounder for brewers at any level of experience.
