Escarpment Labs' Kölsch Ale is a liquid ale yeast that produces a crisp, clean beer with moderate alcohol content. It has an attenuation of 75.0%, medium flocculation, and a medium alcohol tolerance. The ideal fermentation temperature for this ale is 18.0-21.0°C, and it goes well with light to moderately hopped beers such as pilsners, lagers, and blonde ales. Kölsch Ale from Escarpment Labs produces a light-bodied beer with a slightly fruity aroma, making it perfect for those looking for a refreshing beverage on a hot summer day!
