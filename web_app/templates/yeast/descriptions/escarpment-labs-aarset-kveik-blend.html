The Årset Kveik Blend from Escarpment Labs is a liquid ale yeast blend perfect for producing a variety of beer styles. It has high attenuation at 75%, flocculation and alcohol tolerance, making it ideal for creating beers with full body and flavor. The ideal fermentation temperature for the Årset Kveik Blend is between 20-37°C, which makes it suitable for fermenting in warmer environments as well as colder ones. This blend is an excellent choice to craft a wide range of ales with complex layers of flavors.
