The Marina Russian Farmhouse liquid ale yeast from Escarpment Labs is a great choice for beers with a rich, full flavor profile. This yeast has a low flocculation and high attenuation rate of 72%, making it perfect for producing dry beers. The alcohol tolerance is medium, meaning it can handle higher levels of alcohol without compromising on flavor or fermentation quality. It ferments best at a temperature range of 25-39 degrees Celsius, allowing brewers to create complex yet balanced flavors that are sure to please beer drinkers.
