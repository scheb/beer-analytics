Bergamot hops are a relatively new addition to the brewing world, but they have quickly gained popularity among brewers for their unique flavor and aroma profile.

Bergamot hops are known for their distinctive citrusy and floral aroma with hints of spice and a slight bitterness. They are often used in pale ales, IPAs, and other hop-forward styles of beer to add a unique layer of complexity to the brew. Bergamot hops are often used in combination with other hop varieties to create a well-rounded flavor and aroma profile.
