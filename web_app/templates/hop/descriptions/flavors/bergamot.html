Bergamot flavor in beer hops derives from the citrusy, floral, and slightly spicy characteristics reminiscent of bergamot fruit. This unique flavor profile is highly sought after in various beer styles, particularly in IPAs, Saisons, and Belgian Witbiers. IPAs benefit from bergamot's citrusy notes, enhancing the overall hop profile. Saisons, known for their spicy and fruity traits, are complemented by bergamot's zest. Belgian Witbiers, with their delicate and refreshing nature, are elevated by the infusion of bergamot's aromatic essence. Overall, bergamot flavor adds a refreshing complexity to these beer styles, enriching the drinking experience.
