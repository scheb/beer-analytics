Hops with pear flavor are a relatively new addition to the world of brewing, and their use in beer styles is still evolving. However, they are generally considered desirable in certain styles where fruitiness is a key characteristic. One such style is Belgian Saison, which is known for its fruity and spicy notes. The addition of hops with pear flavor can complement and enhance these characteristics, creating a complex and refreshing beer. Another style where these hops can be desirable is American Pale Ale, as they can add a unique twist to the citrus and floral notes typically associated with the style. Overall, hops with pear flavor can be a great addition to any beer style where fruity or spicy notes are prominent.
