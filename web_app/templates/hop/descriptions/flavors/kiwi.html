Kiwi hops are a relatively new variety of hop that have become popular in craft beer brewing. They impart a unique flavor and aroma, with notes of tropical fruit, citrus, and even a hint of kiwi. These hops are desirable in a variety of beer styles, including IPAs, pale ales, pilsners, and wheat beers. The flavor profile of Kiwi hops is complex and can add an interesting layer to many different styles of beer. They can be used for bittering or for late-addition aroma and flavor additions.
