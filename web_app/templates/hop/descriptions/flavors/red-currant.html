Red Currant is a flavor found in certain hops. It has a tart, fruity taste with hints of raspberry and blackberry that can add complexity to various beer styles. Red Currant hops are often used in Belgian ales, wheat beers, and sour beers to provide a unique flavor profile. It is also desirable in pale ales, IPAs, and stouts for its subtle sweetness and earthy notes. Red Currant hops can be used as an aroma or flavor hop to create a balanced beer with a pleasant finish.
