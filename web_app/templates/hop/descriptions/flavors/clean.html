"Clean" as a beer hops flavor refers to a crisp, refreshing, and well-balanced taste that doesn't overwhelm the palate. In the context of hops, it implies that the bitterness is present but not overpowering, allowing for the subtle nuances of the malt and other ingredients to come through. Clean hops flavors often exhibit mild to moderate floral, herbal, or earthy notes, without aggressive or lingering bitterness. Beers with clean hops flavors are typically easy to drink and highly sessionable, making them popular among a wide range of beer enthusiasts. These characteristics are typically achieved by carefully selecting hop varieties and adjusting the brewing process to create a harmonious blend of flavors and aromas.
