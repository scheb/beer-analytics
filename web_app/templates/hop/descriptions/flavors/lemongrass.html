Hops with lemongrass flavor can add a refreshing and zesty twist to a variety of beer styles, particularly those with citrus or herbal notes. Some popular beer styles that pair well with Lemongrass hops include Belgian Saisons, American Pale Ales, IPAs, and Witbiers. Lemongrass can complement the citrus and fruity flavors in these beers, while also enhancing the spicy and floral characteristics of the hops. Lemongrass can also add a unique twist to sour and wild ales, providing a bright and herbaceous flavor profile that can balance the tartness of the beer. Ultimately, the use of Lemongrass hops in beer depends on the brewer's taste preferences and the desired flavor profile of the beer, but they can be a great addition to a range of styles.
