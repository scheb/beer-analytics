Whitegrape is a fruity, floral hop variety with a distinct white grape flavor. It has a low bitterness and high aroma, making it an ideal choice for beer styles such as IPAs, pale ales, and Belgian-style ales. Its unique flavor profile adds a complex and refreshing taste to these beers, making them more enjoyable and interesting to drink. Whitegrape hops are also great for adding a subtle sweetness to wheat beers, lagers, and pilsners.
