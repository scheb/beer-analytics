Hops with green tea flavor can be a desirable addition to certain beer styles. Generally, lighter beer styles such as pale ales, IPAs, and lagers can benefit from the addition of green tea hops. The green tea flavor can add a unique and refreshing twist to the bitterness and aroma provided by traditional hops. Additionally, the delicate flavor of green tea can complement the light and crisp body of these beer styles. However, it's important to note that the addition of green tea hops should be done carefully to avoid overwhelming the other flavors and aromas in the beer. Overall, hops with green tea flavor can be a great addition to certain beer styles, particularly those that are lighter and more delicate in flavor.
