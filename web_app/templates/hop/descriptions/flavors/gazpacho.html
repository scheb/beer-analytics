Gazpacho is a unique hop variety that has a distinct flavor profile of citrus, melon, and white pepper. It is desirable in a range of beer styles, such as IPAs, Pale Ales, and Wheat beers. Gazpacho hops provide a tropical fruit aroma and a hint of spice to the beer. It can be used for bittering or late additions to add complexity to the flavor profile. This hop is perfect for those looking for an interesting twist on their favorite beer styles.
