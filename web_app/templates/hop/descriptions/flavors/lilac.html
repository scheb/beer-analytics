Lilac is a popular flavor with a distinct floral and fruity aroma. It's often used in pale ales, IPAs, and Belgian-style beers. Its delicate flavor adds a unique sweetness that can be used to balance out the bitterness of other hops. It also pairs well with citrusy hop varieties, creating an interesting blend of flavors. For those looking for an extra hint of floral flavor in their brews, Lilac is a great choice.
