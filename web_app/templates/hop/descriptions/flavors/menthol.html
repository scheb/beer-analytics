Menthol flavors in hops can add a unique, refreshing twist to certain beer styles. These cool, minty notes are often found in hops such as Polaris and Northern Brewer. While not common in traditional beer styles, menthol characteristics can be desirable in experimental and craft brews, such as Stouts, Porters, and Winter Warmers. The menthol nuances can complement the rich, roasted malt profiles of these beers, providing a refreshing contrast and creating a balanced, intriguing experience for the discerning beer enthusiast.
