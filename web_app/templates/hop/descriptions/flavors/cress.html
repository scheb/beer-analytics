Cress is a hop variety that imparts a unique flavor to beer. It has a strong, spicy, and herbal aroma with notes of citrus, pine, and black pepper. It is often used in pale ales and IPAs, as well as Belgian-style ales and other styles that benefit from its intense aromatics. Cress hops can also be used to add complexity to darker beers, such as stouts and porters. Its spicy and herbal qualities make it an ideal choice for adding depth to any beer recipe.
