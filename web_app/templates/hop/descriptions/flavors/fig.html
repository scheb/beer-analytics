Fig flavor in hops is a unique and interesting addition to many craft beers. It adds a subtle sweetness and earthy tones that can really enhance the overall flavor of the beer. This flavor is most desirable in Belgian styles, such as Witbiers, Saisons, and Belgian Ales. It can also be used to add complexity to IPAs, Porters, and Stouts. Fig flavor in hops can be a great way to add depth and character to any beer style when used correctly.
