Jasmine is a popular hop flavor used in beer brewing. It has a unique floral and spicy aroma that adds complexity to the flavor profile of the beer. Jasmine hops are often used in Pale Ales, IPAs, and Belgian-style beers due to its ability to add a subtle yet distinct flavor. The floral and spicy notes of jasmine hops can also be combined with other hop varieties to create more complex flavors. Jasmine hops are a great way to add a unique and interesting flavor to your favorite beer styles.
