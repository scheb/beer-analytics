Citrus flavor hops are typically associated with various beer styles, particularly those that highlight hop character and fruity aroma profiles. Some of the most common styles featuring citrus hops include American Pale Ales, India Pale Ales (IPAs), New England IPAs, and American Wheat Beers. These styles often showcase bright, refreshing, and fruity flavors, with grapefruit, orange, and lemon being the most prevalent citrus notes. The use of citrus hops in these beer styles creates a perfect balance between bitterness and fruity aroma, making them popular among craft beer enthusiasts.
