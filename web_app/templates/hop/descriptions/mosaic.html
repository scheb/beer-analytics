Mosaic is a popular variety of hops used in brewing beer. It was first released in 2012 by the Hop Breeding Company, and has quickly become a favorite among brewers for its complex flavor profile and versatility.

Mosaic hops are known for their strong aroma and flavor of tropical fruits, such as mango, pineapple, and passionfruit, as well as notes of citrus and pine. They also have a high alpha acid content, making them a great choice for bittering and flavoring in a wide range of beer styles, from IPAs to pale ales.

Overall, Mosaic hops are a fantastic choice for adding bold, fruity flavors and aromas to beer. They have quickly become a staple in the craft beer industry, and are a popular choice among homebrewers as well.
