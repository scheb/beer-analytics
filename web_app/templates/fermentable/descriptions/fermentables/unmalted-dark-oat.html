Unmalted Dark Oat is a versatile grain that adds depth and complexity to beer brewing. With its distinct flavor profile of roasted oats, it imparts a smooth nutty taste with hints of chocolate and coffee. This unique characteristic greatly influences the overall taste of the beer, providing richness and enhancing mouthfeel. Typically used as an adjunct in brewing, Unmalted Dark Oat brings several desirable properties to the table. Firstly, it contributes to improved head retention due to its high protein content. Additionally, it enhances body and creaminess while also adding a touch of silky texture to the finished product.

Due to its dark color and bold flavors, Unmalted Dark Oat is commonly utilized in styles such as stouts, porters, brown ales, or any beers aiming for additional malt complexity. Its inclusion can result in well-rounded brews that exhibit deep roasty notes alongside a velvety smoothness on the palate.
